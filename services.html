Our Physiotherapy Services

We provide specialized treatment for a wide range of orthopedic, neurological, and sports-related conditions.

Orthopedic Physiotherapy

Expert care for bone, joint, and muscle problems. We help you move without pain.

  • Back Pain Treatment: Relief from lower back pain, Sciatica, and Slip Disc.
  • Neck Pain (Spondylosis): Treatment for cervical pain and stiffness.
  • Knee Pain (Osteoarthritis): Strengthening exercises and pain management.
  • Frozen Shoulder: Increasing range of motion and reducing stiffness.

Neurological Rehabilitation

Specialized therapy for nervous system disorders to improve functional independence.

  • Paralysis / Hemiplegia: Regaining muscle control after stroke.
  • Stroke Rehabilitation: Comprehensive recovery plans covering mobility and balance.
  • Parkinson’s Disease: Managing tremors and improving gait/walking.
  • Nerve Injuries: Treatment for Bell’s Palsy and other nerve conditions.

Post-Surgery Rehabilitation

Critically important for full recovery after major surgeries.

  • Total Knee Replacement (TKR): Regain walking ability and knee bending.
  • Hip Replacement: Safe mobilization exercises.
  • Ligament Surgeries (ACL/PCL): Sports-specific rehabilitation to return to play.
  • Fracture Rehabilitation: Restoring strength after plaster removal.

Geriatric / Elderly Care

Gentle and effective care for senior citizens to maintain an active lifestyle.

  • Balance Training: Preventing falls and fractures.
  • General Weakness: Improving overall strength and energy.
  • Arthritis Management: Reducing joint pain to improve daily living.
